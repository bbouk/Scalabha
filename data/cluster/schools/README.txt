Achievement Test Scores, New Haven Schools

Measurements at the beginning of the 4th grade (when the national average is 4.0) and of the 6th grade in 25 schools in New Haven. 

Variables:

        4th grade reading
        4th grade arithmetic
        6th grade reading
        6th grade arithmetic 

So, a line like 

BARNARD            3.9         3.8        5.9        6.2 

means that Barnard school had a 3.9 for 4th graders' reading, 3.8 for 4th graders' arithmetic, 5.9 for 6th graders' reading, and 6.2 for 6th graders' arithmetic.

Data obtained from: http://www.uni-koeln.de/themen/statistik/data/cluster/
